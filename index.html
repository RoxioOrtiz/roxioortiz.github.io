My Completely Objective (and Totally Subjective) Ranking

Top 10 Taylor Swift Songs

This is a highly curated list of some of the best songs across Taylor's discography. They are all masterpieces, but some are more *masterpiece* than others.

  1. All Too Well (10 Minute Version) (Taylor's Version) (From The Vault) - The lyrical magnum opus.
  2. Cruel Summer - An absolute synth-pop banger and the perfect summer heartbreak anthem.
  3. Style - Pure, driving, iconic 1989 perfection.
  4. You Belong With Me - The classic, essential Taylor Swift narrative.
  5. Getaway Car - A thrilling story and a highlight from Reputation.
  6. August - The emotional, nostalgic core of the folklore love triangle.
  7. Dear John - A brutally honest and emotionally devastating acoustic track.
  8. Blank Space - Sharp, witty, and a brilliant deconstruction of her public image.
  9. New Romantics - The ultimate feel-good, 'shaking it off' anthem.
  10. Champagne Problems - A beautifully tragic, cinematic story from evermore.
